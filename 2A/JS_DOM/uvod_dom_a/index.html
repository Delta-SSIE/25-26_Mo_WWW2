The Amazing Platypus

A truly unique mammal

About

The platypus is a semi-aquatic mammal native to Australia. It is one of the few mammals that lays eggs instead of giving birth to live young.

Interesting Facts

  • It has a duck-like bill.
  • It lays eggs but produces milk.
  • Males have venomous spurs.
  • It can detect electric signals from prey.

Habitat

Platypuses live in rivers, lakes, and streams. They build burrows in riverbanks and are excellent swimmers.
