Porcupines

Spiky but peaceful forest animals

About Porcupines

Porcupines are large rodents known for their sharp quills. They live in forests, deserts, and grasslands. When threatened, they raise their quills to protect themselves.

Interesting Facts

  • Porcupines cannot shoot their quills.
  • They are mostly nocturnal animals.
  • They eat plants, bark, and leaves.
  • Their quills help protect them from predators.

Habitat

Porcupines are found in North and South America, Africa, and parts of Asia. They usually live in trees or burrows.
