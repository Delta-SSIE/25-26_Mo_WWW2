About Dolphins

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and social nature. They belong to the family of toothed whales and are found worldwide, mostly in shallow seas of continental shelves.

Fun Facts

🧠 Dolphins are extremely intelligent and can solve complex problems.
🗣️ They communicate using clicks, whistles, and body movements.
🌊 Dolphins can swim up to 60 km/h (37 mph).
👥 They live in groups called pods.

Habitat

Dolphins live in oceans and seas around the world. Some species also live in rivers. They prefer warm tropical waters but can also be found in colder regions.
