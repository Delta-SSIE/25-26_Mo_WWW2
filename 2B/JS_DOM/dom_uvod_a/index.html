About Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds that live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere. They are excellent swimmers and spend a lot of time in the ocean.

Penguins are flightless birds that live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere. They are excellent swimmers and spend a lot of time in the ocean.

Where Do They Live?

Most penguins live in Antarctica, but some species live in warmer places like South Africa and South America.

What Do Penguins Eat?

Penguins eat fish, squid, and krill. They catch their food while swimming underwater.
